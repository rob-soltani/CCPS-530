"Marques Keith Brownlee (/mɑːrˈkɛz ˈkiːθ ˈbraʊnliː/ mar-KEZ KEITH BROWN-lee; born December 3, 1993), also known professionally as MKBHD, is an American YouTuber and professional ultimate frisbee player, best known for his technology-focused videos and podcast. The name of his YouTube channel is a concatenation of MKB (Brownlee's initials) and HD (for high-definition)."
